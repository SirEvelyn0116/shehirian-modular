What is FSSC 22000?

FSSC 22000 (Food Safety System Certification) is a robust, ISO-based food safety management system certification scheme. It provides a framework for effectively managing food safety responsibilities and is fully recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI).

Built on the foundation of ISO 22000, FSSC 22000 integrates the ISO 9001 quality management approach with HACCP principles and sector-specific prerequisite programs. This comprehensive standard is used by over 20,000 certified organizations across the entire food supply chain, from farming to retail.

  • Risk-Based Thinking: Proactive identification and control of food safety hazards
  • Communication Excellence: Clear internal and external food safety communication
  • Legal Compliance: Full adherence to food safety regulations and requirements

The FSSC 22000 Framework

FSSC 22000 combines multiple internationally recognized standards into one comprehensive certification:

  • ISO 22000: Core food safety management system requirements
  • ISO/TS 22002-1: Prerequisite programs for food manufacturing
  • Additional FSSC Requirements: Service management, product labeling, food defense, and fraud mitigation

This multi-layered approach ensures that every aspect of our bulgur wheat production meets stringent international standards.
