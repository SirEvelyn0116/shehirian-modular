What is IFS Food?

The IFS (International Featured Standards) Food Standard is a GFSI-recognized food safety and quality standard developed by European retailers to ensure the safety and quality of food products. With over 25,000 certified companies worldwide, IFS Food is particularly important for suppliers to European retail chains and food service companies.

Originally created by German, French, and Italian retail federations, IFS Food has become a globally recognized benchmark for food safety management, focusing on product safety, quality, legality, and authenticity throughout the supply chain.

Shehirian's IFS Food Certification

Our IFS Food certification underscores Shehirian's commitment to meeting the rigorous standards demanded by European and international markets. This certification demonstrates:

  • Product Safety: Comprehensive controls ensuring every batch of bulgur wheat is safe for consumption
  • Quality Assurance: Consistent product specifications and customer satisfaction
  • Process Control: Documented and verified manufacturing procedures
  • Regulatory Compliance: Full adherence to food laws and regulations
  • Authenticity: Protection against food fraud and mislabeling

The IFS Food Structure

IFS Food is organized into six major sections that cover all aspects of food production:

  • Senior Management Responsibility: Leadership commitment and corporate policy
  • Quality and Food Safety Management System: HACCP implementation and documentation
  • Resource Management: Personnel hygiene, training, and facility management
  • Planning and Production Process: Product development, purchasing, and manufacturing controls
  • Measurements, Analysis, Improvements: Internal audits and continuous improvement
  • Food Defense and External Inspections: Security and fraud prevention measures
