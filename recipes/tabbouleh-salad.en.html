Tabbouleh Salad

Category: Salad

Cuisine: Middle Eastern

Servings: 6 servings

Prep Time: 1 hour 15 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Total: 1 hour 20 minutes

Description

A refreshing Middle Eastern salad with bulgur wheat, fresh vegetables, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup bulgur wheat
  • 1 cucumber peeled and diced
  • 2 green onions thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup diced green peppers
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons minced parsley
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped mint or 1/4 teaspoon dry mint

Instructions

  1. Combine 2 cups boiling water and 1 cup bulgur wheat.
  2. Let it stand until all water is absorbed, about 1 hour.
  3. Combine in salad bowl with remaining ingredients.
  4. Toss until dressing is well distributed.
