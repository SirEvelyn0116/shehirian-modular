Scotch Broth with Bulgur Wheat

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Scottish

Servings: 6-8 servings

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 1 hour 45 minutes | Total: 2 hours

Description

A traditional Scottish soup transformed with hearty bulgur wheat and lamb.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. lamb shoulder
  • 8 cups water
  • 1/2 cup bulgur wheat
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced turnip
  • 1 cup chopped leeks
  • 1/2 cup pearl barley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add lamb and water. Bring to a boil and skim off any foam.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour until lamb is tender.
  3. Remove lamb, shred meat, and return to pot.
  4. Add bulgur wheat, barley, carrots, turnip, and leeks.
  5. Simmer for 45 minutes until grains and vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
