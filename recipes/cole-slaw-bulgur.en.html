Cole Slaw with Bulgur

Category: Salad | Cuisine: American | Servings: 8-10 servings | Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Description

A hearty twist on classic coleslaw with the addition of nutritious bulgur wheat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur wheat (fine or medium)
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling water over bulgur wheat and let stand for 10 minutes until tender. Drain any excess water and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooled bulgur, shredded cabbage, and shredded carrots.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss until well coated. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
