Bulgur Garden Salad

Category: Salad | Cuisine: American | Servings: 8 servings | Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

Description

A versatile garden salad with bulgur wheat, fresh vegetables, and hard-boiled eggs — perfect as a side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups bulgur wheat (medium)
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 1 head lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup sliced radishes
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1 cup Italian or vinaigrette dressing

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling water over bulgur wheat and let stand for 15 minutes until tender. Drain any excess water and cool completely.
  2. In a large serving bowl, combine lettuce, diced tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, radishes, and shredded carrots.
  3. Add cooled bulgur wheat to the vegetable mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Top with sliced hard-boiled eggs and serve with your favorite dressing.
