Armenian Style Lentil Soup

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Armenian

Servings: 4 servings

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 1 hour 15 minutes | Total: 1 hour 25 minutes

Description

A hearty soup with lentils, mint, and parsley — a Shehirian family classic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup lentils
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint

Instructions

  1. Boil lentils in water for 15 minutes.
  2. Add onion, parsley, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 45 minutes.
  3. Add mint and butter. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add 1/4 tsp. pepper and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Optional: Fry butter until light brown and pour over soup.
