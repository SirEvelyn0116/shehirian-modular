Royal Soup

A lemony meatball soup with rice and parsley, inspired by Middle Eastern flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 gallon chicken broth
  • 1/2 lb. ground beef (not too lean)
  • 1/4 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup very finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Slice of 2 lemons

Steps

  1. Mix raw meat, bulghur wheat, onion, parsley and seasoning together and shape into 1" diameter balls.
  2. Add rice to the meat balls mixture.
  3. Add meat balls to hot chicken broth and simmer until cooked through.
  4. Add lemon juice and celery. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add salt and lemon slices. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Adjust sourness, saltiness, or thickness with sugar, water, or rice as needed.
  7. Pour broth over meatballs and serve.
