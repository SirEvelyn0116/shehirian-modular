Bulgur Carrot Pineapple Salad

Category: Salad | Cuisine: American | Servings: 8-10 servings | Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Description

A sweet and savory salad combining bulgur wheat with shredded carrots and tropical pineapple.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur wheat (fine or medium)
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling water over bulgur wheat and let stand for 15 minutes until tender. Drain any excess water and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooled bulgur, shredded carrots, and drained crushed pineapple.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, and lemon juice until smooth.
  4. Pour the dressing over the bulgur mixture and toss until well coated. Chill before serving.
