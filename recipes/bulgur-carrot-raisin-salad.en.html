Bulgur Carrot Raisin Salad

Category: Salad | Cuisine: American | Servings: 8-10 servings | Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Description

A variation with raisins and French dressing for a delightful sweet and tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur wheat (fine or medium)
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup French dressing
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling water over bulgur wheat and let stand for 15 minutes until tender. Drain any excess water and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooled bulgur, shredded carrots, and raisins.
  3. Pour French dressing over the mixture and toss until well combined.
  4. Season with salt to taste. Chill before serving for best flavor.
