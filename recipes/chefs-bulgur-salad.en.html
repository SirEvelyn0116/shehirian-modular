Chef's Bulgur Salad

Category: Salad | Cuisine: American | Servings: 8 servings | Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 35 minutes

Description

A hearty main course salad featuring bulgur wheat, fresh vegetables, and your choice of cold meats and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups bulgur wheat (medium)
  • 4 cups boiling water
  • 1 head lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1 cup sliced radishes
  • 2 cups diced cooked ham or turkey
  • 1 cup diced cheese (cheddar or Swiss)
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, quartered
  • 1 cup ranch or Italian dressing

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling water over bulgur wheat and let stand for 15 minutes until tender. Drain any excess water and cool completely.
  2. In a large serving bowl, arrange lettuce as a base. Top with cooled bulgur wheat.
  3. Arrange cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, radishes, diced ham or turkey, and diced cheese over the bulgur.
  4. Garnish with quartered hard-boiled eggs. Serve with your favorite dressing on the side.
