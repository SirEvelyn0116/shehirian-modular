Bulgur Dutch Cucumber Salad

Category: Salad | Cuisine: Dutch | Servings: 6-8 servings | Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

Description

A creamy and refreshing salad with bulgur, cucumbers, radishes, and a tangy sour cream dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur wheat (fine or medium)
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 large cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup sliced radishes
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dill weed
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling water over bulgur wheat and let stand for 15 minutes until tender. Drain any excess water and cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooled bulgur, sliced cucumbers, and sliced radishes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together sour cream, white vinegar, sugar, salt, dill weed, and black pepper until smooth.
  4. Pour the dressing over the bulgur mixture and toss gently. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
